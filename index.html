Product Overview

Evident

Evidence-backed growth

Press → or swipe to begin

The Problem

Great work disappears

People forget to capture wins and scramble for proof at review time. Managers lack concrete evidence and coaching artifacts.

😵

Review-time panic

Scrambling to remember six months of achievements the night before

😶

Manager blind spots

1:1s become status updates because there's no shared evidence

😬

Invisible work

Code reviews, unblocking, coordination — never captured anywhere

The Solution

Evident turns everyday work into clear, evidence-backed growth

  • Captures "proof of work" from the tools you already use
  • Keeps everything private by default
  • Requires your approval for what counts
  • Links proof of work to goals
  • Turns approved items into simple summaries for 1:1s and reviews
Core Principle
Private-only until you choose to share.

Evident is built to feel safe. It does not "report on" people automatically.

Who It's For

User stories

💻

For an employee

I want the important things I do to be captured automatically, so I can confidently show my impact in 1:1s and reviews without having to remember everything.

🤝

For a manager

I want a clear, shareable view of achievements against goals, so our 1:1s focus on growth and blockers — not surveillance.

Feature 1

Your Inbox

A single list of items that may be worth counting as "proof of work."

  • Draft "this might matter" — captured automatically or added by you
  • Approved "yes, this counts" — you decide
  • Discarded "no, ignore this" — keeps things clean

Noise never becomes "performance tracking." You stay in control.

Feature 2

Turn activity into impact

A details page where you make each item clear and meaningful.

  • Edit the title and summary
  • Add an impact note — plain English: why it mattered
  • Add or remove links to the work (Jira, GitHub, etc.)
  • Approve or Discard with explicit actions

Automatic capture can't explain value — your short note does.

Feature 3

Goals

A short list of outcomes that matter — projects, targets, skills to grow.

  • Create and edit goals
  • Tag evidence to one or more goals
  • Easily answer: "What have I done toward Goal X?"

Turns scattered work into a clear story of progress.

Feature 4

Automatic capture

Evident automatically adds draft items when real work happens.

📄 Jira issue → Done
⟶
Draft item created
🐦 GitHub PR merged
⟶
Draft item created

Important: Automatically captured items are always draft-only — never auto-approved.
